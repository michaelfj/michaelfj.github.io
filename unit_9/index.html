Is your body at war with mould?


Our title 'mould wars' refers to the state a body enters into when its immune system takes on an established mould infection. When a person has weakened immunity, the pockets of toxic fungi in the body start to breed very quickly to enlarge their colonies.

When immunity is restored these fungi divert their resources into self-protection rather than multiplying their numbers. The fungi start to wage war against the white blood cells with a brace of toxic chemicals.

The life of lemons


Ever noticed when you buy a bag of lemons that one or two of them go mouldy very quickly but the others next to them are not affected? It is the same with people. Some of us are prone to mould because our immunity is faulty while others never seem to get sick. It can be a problem in your DNA or it might just be your diet or environment.
